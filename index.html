About KapitBaryo

A Community-Centered Online Barangay Service Platform

Our Mission

Modernize local services by offering direct online interfaces for handling processing requirements, improving transaction workflows, lowering crowd sizes inside offices, and structuring public transparency.

Our Vision

To establish standard government technology framework ecosystems across barangays globally, promoting immediate updates, accountability, and robust digital integration.

Digital Transformation Transformation Framework Goals

1. Transition manual, traditional tracking protocols over to automated digital ledger solutions safely stored via web clients.

2. Give residents a voice directly via public-facing concern registers.
